How It Works

Our proprietary 5-step process transforms waste into world-class construction materials.

The Process

From Waste to Wonder in 5 Steps

1

🏭 Waste Sourcing & Collection

We partner with manufacturing plants, power stations, construction companies, and municipal waste facilities to source industrial byproducts like fly ash, slag, mine tailings, and demolition debris. Our logistics network ensures efficient collection with minimal transportation footprint.

2

🔬 Sorting & Material Analysis

Incoming waste materials undergo rigorous laboratory testing to determine composition, contaminant levels, and suitability. Advanced sorting systems separate usable material from non-recyclable elements, ensuring only the highest quality inputs enter our production line.

3

⚙️ Processing & Formulation

Approved materials are processed — crushed, ground, and blended with natural binders using our proprietary formulations. Each brick type has a unique mix design optimized for specific structural and thermal performance characteristics.

4

🧱 Brick Formation & Curing

The blended material is fed into high-pressure hydraulic presses that form precise, uniform bricks. Unlike traditional methods using high-temperature kilns, our cold-press and steam-curing process uses 60% less energy while achieving superior strength.

5

✅ Quality Assurance & Delivery

Every batch undergoes comprehensive testing: compressive strength, water absorption, dimensional accuracy, and efflorescence resistance. IS-certified results are documented for each shipment. Finished bricks are palletized and delivered directly to your project site.
